The Initiatives That Have Been Taken

Free blood group diagnosis and donor data collection

If the blood group is known, a person can easily donate blood. A healthy person can donate blood every four months. It does not harm the body. Moreover, blood cannot be taken without knowing the blood test or group.
The biggest advantage of collecting blood donor information is that blood donors can be found easily.

Wash Your Hand

Touching something can transfer germs to every surface of the hand. Wash your hands as soon as possible.

Stand Up Against Rapists

Rapist is one of the most terrible crimes on earth and it happens every few minutes. We stand up against rape culture, victim-blaming and victim-shamming.

Spraying medicine To Destroy The Virus

The virus is attacking people day by day. People are getting sick. Measures have been taken to destroy the virus by spraying the medicine as much as possible in all areas.
